## VISUAL STYLE GUIDE

### Color Palette
- **Background**: Deep space black (#0A0A0A)
- **Accent 1**: Electric blue (#0066FF)
- **Accent 2**: Cyan glow (#00D4FF)
- **Text**: Pure white (#FFFFFF)
- **Highlights**: Neon green (#00FF88) for success states

### Typography
- **Main font**: Inter Bold for titles
- **Body font**: Inter Regular
- **Code/Tech**: JetBrains Mono for technical details

### Animation Style
- **Fast cuts** for mystery building
- **Smooth transitions** for reveals
- **Glitch effects** for impossibility moments
- **Particle effects** for ZK proof visualizations
- **Glow effects** around commitments/hashes

### Sound Design
- **Background**: Low electronic hum
- **Mystery moments**: Suspenseful strings
- **Reveal**: Ascending synth
- **Technical explanations**: Clean tech sounds
- **Call to action**: Confident beat drop
