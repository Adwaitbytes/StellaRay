## 🎥 PRODUCTION NOTES

### Visual Style
- **Dark theme** with green accents (#00FF9F)
- **Smooth transitions** between screens
- **Minimal text overlays** - let the demo speak
- **Screen recordings** in high quality (1080p minimum)

### Pacing
- **Fast-paced** - no dead air
- **Show, don't tell** - demo features live
- **Real interactions** - actual clicks, not animations
- **Smooth cuts** - no jarring transitions

### Key Moments to Highlight
1. **0:45** - The one-click Google sign-in
2. **1:15** - Wallet appears (the "wow" moment)
3. **1:30** - Console showing undefined email (privacy proof)
4. **2:15** - 94% cost reduction (the business case)
5. **2:45** - Streaming payment live (unique feature)

### Screen Recording Tips
1. **Clear your browser** - no extra tabs or bookmarks
2. **Use Incognito mode** - clean Google login screen
3. **Prepare test account** - have Google credentials ready
4. **Pre-load pages** - no waiting for loading
5. **Record in 1080p** - crisp, clear text
6. **Use cursor highlighting** - help viewers follow

### Background Music
- **Tempo:** 120-130 BPM
- **Style:** Tech, minimal, modern
- **Volume:** Low enough to hear narrator clearly
- **No lyrics** - instrumental only

### Voiceover Tips
- **Tone:** Confident, clear, not overly excited
- **Pace:** Moderate - not rushed, not slow
- **Emphasis:** On numbers (94%, $0.03, 3 lines)
- **Pauses:** After key stats, let them sink in
- **Energy:** Professional but enthusiastic
